Embedded definition

Embedded System by definition, is also known as an integrated system due to its combination of hardware and software. It comprises intelligent computing devices that are surrounding us with day-to-day life. The devices may be a smartphone, smart watches, smart home devices, medical equipment, security alarms, IoT products, etc., Speciality is connecting all our subjects into a single platform. Mathematics is foremost for signal processing and communication, Programming C to develop Software, leading communication technology, Micro-Controller, Real-Time Operating Systems, Radio Frequency and so on. If you have any questions related to embedded. Please feel free to post questions and we are ready to answer.
